Kahor intaadan bilaabin koorsadan, hubi inaad haysato waxyaabahan muhiimka ah. Qayb kasta waxay kaa caawin doontaa inaad si fiican uga faa'iidaysato casharrada.

---

## Computer ama Tablet

Computer ama Tablet waxay kuu sahlaysaa inaad:

- Si cad u aragto charts-ka
- Si fudud ugu tababarto trading platforms
- Isla markaana u furato Notes-ka iyo Video-ga

<Note>
  **Waxaa lagugula talinayaa.** Casharrada qaarkood waxay ku jiraan charts, trading platforms, iyo falanqayn u baahan
  shaashad weyn.
</Note>

## Mobile Phone

Haddii aadan haysan Computer ama Tablet, waxaad isticmaali kartaa **Mobile Phone**.

Waxaad ku daawan kartaa casharrada, akhrisan kartaa Notes-ka, isla markaana samayn kartaa qayb ka mid ah layliyada.

<Warning>
  Waayo-aragnimada ugu fiican waxay ku jirtaa isticmaalka Computer ama Tablet. Mobile waxay ku habboon tahay daawashada
  iyo akhriska, laakiin charts iyo tababarka platform-ka way adkaan karaan.
</Warning>

---

## Waqti iyo joogteyn

Trading waa xirfad la barto, mana aha wax lagu barto hal maalin.

Si aad uga faa'iidaysato koorsadan:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sida aad waqtiga ugu maamusho" defaultOpen>
    - U qorshee waqti joogto ah oo waxbarasho - Ha degdegin inaad casharrada iska dhaafto - Dib u eeg Notes-ka mar kasta
    oo loo baahdo - Samee Exercise kasta si aad aqoonta ugu beddesho xirfad
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>Xitaa haddii aad haysato **30–60 daqiiqo maalintii**, joogteynta ayaa ka muhiimsan xawaaraha.</Note>

## Internet deggan

Maadaama casharradu ay ka kooban yihiin Video Lessons iyo Docs, waxaad u baahan tahay internet deggan si aad si fudud ugu daawato muuqaalada una akhrido Notes-ka.

<Info>
  Hubi in xiriirkaagu uu xooggan yahay ka hor intaadan bilaabin cashar video ah, si aadan u joojin bartamaha casharka.
</Info>

---

## Maskax furan

Waxa ugu muhiimsan ee aad u baahan tahay waa rabitaan aad ku barato xirfad cusub.

Ha ka welwelin haddii aadan hore wax uga aqoon Forex. Koorsadan waxaa loo dhisay si tallaabo-tallaabo ah, laga bilaabo aasaaska ilaa heerar sare.

<Tip>
  Waxa kaliya ee lagaaga baahan yahay waa: **dulqaad**, **joogteyn**, **tababar**, iyo diyaar u ahaanshaha inaad wax
  cusub barato.
</Tip>
