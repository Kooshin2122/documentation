Ku soo dhawoow **Forex Course**.

Koorsadan waxaa loo diyaariyey qof kasta — laga bilaabo bilowga ilaa qofka doonaya inuu fahamkiisa sii xoojiyo. Ujeeddadeennu ma aha oo keliya inaad barato ereyada Forex, balse waa inaad fahamto sida suuqa u shaqeeyo, sida loo falanqeeyo, iyo sida loo noqdo trader leh qorshe cad iyo maareyn wanaagsan.

Dhamaadka koorsadan waxaad yeelan doontaa aqoon adag oo ku saabsan sida suuqa Forex u shaqeeyo, sida loo sameeyo falanqayn sax ah, iyo sida loo qaato go'aanno ku saleysan xog halkii ay ka ahaan lahaayeen dareen.

## Technical Analysis

Technical Analysis waa barashada dhaqdhaqaaqa qiimaha (Price Action) iyadoo la adeegsanayo charts iyo qalabka falanqaynta.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Waxaad baran doontaa" defaultOpen>
    - Sida loo akhriyo charts - Support & Resistance - Trend iyo Market Structure - Candlestick Patterns - Supply &
    Demand - Liquidity Concepts - Entry & Exit Strategies - Risk Management
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Marka aad dhamayso qaybtan, waxaad awoodi doontaa inaad fahamto waxa uu chart-ku kuu sheegayo iyo meesha ay fursadaha
  ganacsigu ka jiraan.
</Note>

## Trading Psychology

Psychology waa qaybta ugu muhiimsan ee trading-ka. Trader badan waxay yaqaanaan charts, laakiin waxay ku guuldareystaan inay xakameeyaan naftooda.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Waxaad baran doontaa" defaultOpen>
    - Sida loo xakameeyo shucuurta - Fear iyo Greed - Discipline - Patience - Trading Plan - Consistency - Sidee looga
    fogaadaa Overtrading - Sida loo dhiso maskax trader oo xirfad leh
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>Psychology wanaagsan ayaa kaa caawin doonta inaad qorshahaaga raacdo xitaa marka suuqu is beddelo.</Note>

## Sida koorsadu u shaqeyso

Si aad uga faa'iidaysato koorsadan, cashar kasta u raac nidaamkan.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Daawo Video-ga">
    Cashar kasta marka hore daawo muuqaalka (Video Lesson). Video-gu wuxuu sharxayaa fikradda guud isagoo ku tusaya tusaalooyin dhab ah.

    Ha degdegin inaad u gudubto casharka xiga adigoon si fiican u fahmin muuqaalka.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Akhri Notes-ka">
    Kadib markaad daawato video-ga, akhri Notes-ka. Waxay soo koobayaan qodobadii ugu muhiimsanaa waxayna kuu sahlayaan dib-u-eegis degdeg ah.

    Waxaan kugula talineynaa inaad sidoo kale samaysato qoraallo adiga kuu gaar ah.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Dhammaystir Exercise-ka">
    Cashar kasta wuxuu leeyahay Exercise ama Practice. Layliyadani waxay kaa caawinayaan inaad aqoonta u beddesho xirfad.

    Ha ka boodin qaybtan — fahamka dhabta ahi wuxuu yimaadaa markaad gacanta ku tababarto waxaad baratay.

  </Step>
</Steps>
